Mark wants to enter a programming contest that will be held on Thursday at 6 PM. 
This competition will last 4 hours and includes n questions. 
The questions are ordered from easy to difficult.
 So question 1 is the simplest question n the hardest.

Mark, i. knows that solving a question takes i*5 minutes

That same night, Mark is invited to a party.
 Mark has to arrive at the party by 10 PM at the latest. 
The time it takes for Mark to go from home to the party is k minutes.
 The user will enter n (number of questions) and k (time (minutes) to go to the party).
Considering these times, 
Mark can reach the party on time by solving how many questions at most.
